Creates a window, with its top left corner at (xPos, yPos), and its bottom right corner at (xPos + xSize, yPos + ySize).

WindowProc is the main event handler of the program, but it isn't called. spontaneously. Instead, you use it like:

while (HandleMessages(pWindow));
